Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2025

Data Collection

Flapio uses your device's LiDAR sensor and camera to create 3D scans of objects. This data includes point cloud coordinates and color information captured during scanning sessions. All processing happens on your device initially, with optional cloud storage for sharing features.

Data Storage

Your 3D object scans are stored locally on your device by default. When you choose to share a scan using redeem codes, the scan data is uploaded to our secure cloud servers for sharing purposes. You can delete shared scans at any time, which will remove them from our servers.

Camera and Sensor Usage

Flapio requires access to your device's camera and LiDAR sensor for 3D object scanning functionality. The camera is used to capture color information, while the LiDAR sensor creates the 3D point cloud data. These sensors are only active during scanning sessions and require explicit user permission.

Sharing and Redeem Codes

When you generate a redeem code to share a scan, the scan data is uploaded to our servers and made accessible to anyone with the code. Shared scans are stored securely and can be accessed by anyone who has the redeem code. You are responsible for sharing codes only with people you trust.

Data Security

All scan data is encrypted during transmission and storage. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that shared scans are accessible to anyone with the redeem code, so we recommend only sharing codes with trusted individuals.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and features. This data cannot be traced back to individual users and does not include any personal information or scan data.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or data practices, please contact us at privacy@flapio.app
